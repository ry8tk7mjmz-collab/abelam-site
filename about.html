My journey

Earned brick by brick, never inherited.

Abraham Leimzider built his career from ground level through decades of real estate execution, and now opens that same machine to passive investors.

Concrete into capital

Born into real estate.

Abraham bootstrapped his earliest acquisitions using seller-financed deals and creative structuring, starting with minimal capital and negotiating complex terms others walked away from.

He learned resilience the hard way, including through a miszoned property acquisition that taught him to underwrite for what can go wrong, not just what should go right. He scaled from single deals to a 1000-unit portfolio and a 50+ person property management team, operating across North America.
